- Gate 0 baseline coherence pass.
- Gate 1 dual lead depth pass.
- Gate 2 critique actionability pass.
- Gate 3 scorecard and veto pass.

- Workflow drift if old CP-01 priorities keep driving decisions.

- Missing evidence of accept/reject logic in compilation rounds.

## Exit Condition For Planning
- Final packet accepted by operator.
- Winner proposal selected with no unresolved critical risk.
